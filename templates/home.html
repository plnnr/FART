Buy and sell development rights today.

The Floor-Area Ratio Transfer System (FARTS®) safely and securely connects developers looking to transfer development entitlements.

FARTS® is an app that lets property owners sell unused development rights to other property owners of qualifying sites who need additional development entitlements. It's a new way to allow sites to maximize their development potential and find their highest and best use.

Explore development alternatives with greater confidence.

In a dynamic market, FARTS provides your project with greater certainty to develop it as you see fit. It allows for more flexibility within your pro forma to maximize site opportunity. Buy additional FAR when you need it, and sell extra FAR entitlements at a price you specify.
